Main Menu

The main screen offers three options:

  1. Press "Start" to enter the camera view.
  2. Use the on/off switch to enable or disable "debug" mode, in which the image processing sequence is made visible by saving each step of the processing pipeline as a separate image (currently 8 images saved for each photograph taken).
  3. Click "Help" to view this webpage.

Photo Access

To remove your saved images, use the standard "Photos" app that comes with your iPhone. This allows you to download photos to a PC using USB, send them via email, or share them to Facebook, Instagram etc.
